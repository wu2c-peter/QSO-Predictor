## Bug reports and feature requests

Please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/wu2c-peter/qso-predictor/issues).
This is the fastest way to get a response and helps other users find answers
to similar questions.

Before filing an issue, please check:

- [Existing open and closed issues](https://github.com/wu2c-peter/qso-predictor/issues?q=is%3Aissue) — your question may already be answered
- [Troubleshooting wiki](https://github.com/wu2c-peter/QSO-Predictor/wiki/Help-and-Troubleshooting) — common problems and solutions

## Other questions

For private questions or if you don't use GitHub, email **qsop@wu2c.net**.

## Documentation

- [User Guide](/user-guide/) — Getting started, workflow, and interpreting the display
- [Technical Wiki](https://github.com/wu2c-peter/QSO-Predictor/wiki) — Advanced topics, troubleshooting, and internals

## When reporting a problem

Please include:

- **QSO Predictor version** (Help → About)
- **Operating system** and version (Windows 10/11, macOS, Linux)
- **WSJT-X or JTDX version**
- **What you did**, what you expected, and what happened instead
- **Error messages** from the console, if any

This information helps diagnose problems much faster.
