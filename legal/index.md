## About QSO Predictor

QSO Predictor is a free, open-source real-time tactical assistant for FT8 and FT4 amateur radio digital modes. It shows you the band from the DX station's perspective — helping you identify clear frequencies, confirm propagation paths, and time your calls more effectively.

The project is licensed under GPLv3. The complete source code, documentation, and release history are available on GitHub.
